Isolation blogging

As someone who is heavily involved in the digital space, I often find myself learning just as much from my customers as they learn from me. The isolation has for many been a time of hardship and change but for me it has really highlighted that I need to start writing about all the projects I work on as much for my own reflection as it is to serve as a modern resume. In this first blog I'll be going over some of the recent highlights of customers succeeding despite the quarantine and what that's taught me about digital resilience in the age of always on marketing.
