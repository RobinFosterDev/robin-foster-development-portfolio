Isolation blogging

As someone who is heavily involved in the digital space, I often find myself learning just as much from my customers as they learn from me. The isolation has for many been a time of hardship and change but for me it has really highlighted that I need to start writing about all the projects I work on as much for my own reflection as it is to serve as a modern resume. In this first blog I'll be going over some of the recent highlights of customers succeeding despite the quarantine.

Key Findings

  1. Technology is here to stay
  2. The human component
  3. A reduction in spend may mean an investment into Technology

Technology is here to stay

There is nothing like a global pandemic to unite people in engaging with new technology. Throughout the pandemic, we saw the market react to this truth. Solutions like Zoom and Microsoft Teams went from a solution that might be useful in the future to one that needed to be implemented yesterday. Overnight, all of my meetings were updated to include online meeting links and all my flights were cancelled.
